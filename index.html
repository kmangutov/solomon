Informed Consent
Description

We are conducting a research study about survey formats for collecting feedback. All data collected in this study will be used for research purposes only. You will examine some designs and other information and provide a critique and any suggestions for improvement. Participation should take only a few minutes.

Risks and Benefits

The risks of participation are no greater than those associated with everyday life. The benefit is helping to contribute knowledge about feedback quality.

Compensation

You will be compensated $0.35 for your participation in this HIT.

Confidentiality

Your Mechanical Turk Worker ID will be used to distribute payment to you but will not be stored with your survey responses. We will not access any personally identifying information that you may have put on your Amazon profile page. Any reports and presentations about the findings from this study will not include any personally identifying information and will only be reported in aggregate form.

Subject's Rights

Your participation is voluntary. You may stop participating at any time by closing the browser window or the program to withdraw from the study. Partial data will not be analyzed.

Contact

For additional questions about this research, you may contact: Kirill Mangutov (manguto2@illinois.edu), University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign
